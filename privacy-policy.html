Effective: January 1, 2025  ·  Updated:

01

Introduction

Welcome to the personal portfolio of Muhammad Hamza Zia. This Privacy & Security Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect any information you provide when visiting or interacting with this website.

I am committed to your privacy. This policy is written in plain language so you always know exactly what is happening with your data. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

02

Information I Collect

📋

Information You Provide Directly

When you use the contact form you may submit your name, email address, and message. This is sent via Web3Forms directly to my inbox — no submission data is stored on any server I own.

📊

Automatically Collected Data

Standard web data may be collected in aggregated, anonymised form — browser type, device type, approximate region, pages visited, and referring URL. No individual user is identified or tracked.

What I Do NOT Collect

  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Payment or financial information of any kind
  • Government ID or sensitive personal data
  • Precise GPS or real-time location data
  • Social media credentials or account information
  • Any data from minors under the age of 13
03

How Your Information is Used

  • To read and respond to your contact enquiry
  • To understand what type of collaboration you are interested in
  • To improve the content and performance of this portfolio
  • To identify and resolve technical errors

I will never sell your information, share it with advertisers, use it for unsolicited marketing, or pass it to any third party beyond what is described here.

04

Third-Party Services

Web3Forms

Contact form submissions are processed by Web3Forms and forwarded to my email. They do not store submissions permanently. See web3forms.com/privacy.

Google Fonts

Typography is served via Google Fonts CDN. Your browser makes a request to Google's servers on page load. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Font Awesome

Icons are loaded from the Font Awesome CDN. A standard IP-level CDN request is made on load. See fontawesome.com/privacy.

LinkedIn & External Links

Clicking external links takes you off this site — those platforms' own policies apply. I have no control over data collected there.

05

Cookies & Local Storage

This portfolio does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any cross-site cookies.

Local Storage

The contact form uses your browser's localStorage to enforce a 10-submission daily limit, storing only a count and timestamp — no personal information, only on your device, cleared after 24 hours.

Language Preference

Your selected language may be saved in localStorage so it persists across visits. This data never leaves your device.

06

Security

🔒

HTTPS Encryption

All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS, preventing third parties from reading data in transit.

🚫

No Server-Side Storage

This is a fully static website — no database, no user accounts, no backend. Form submissions go directly to my email and are not stored elsewhere.

⚡

Rate Limiting

The contact form enforces 10 submissions per 24 hours via client-side localStorage to protect against automated spam and abuse.

🧹

No Tracking Scripts

No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar, etc.) are installed. No behavioural profiling or cross-site tracking is performed.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact me immediately so I can resolve it promptly.
